Spikeball

A high-energy game built around quick reactions, teamwork, and nonstop movement around the net.

Why People Love It

Spikeball combines the intensity of a rally sport with the freedom to move in every direction. It is easy to learn, fun to play casually, and exciting to watch when the pace picks up.

Game Guide

Spikeball Rules

  • How to Play

    1. 2 vs 2 around a small round net
    2. Hit the ball off the net so the other team cant return it.

  • Basic Rules

    1. Each team gets up to 3 hits to return the ball
    2. The same person can't hit twice in a row
    3. The ball must hit the net, not the rim

  • You lose the Point if:

    1. The ball hits the ground
    2. You miss the net
    3. It hits the rim
    4. Your team uses more than 3 hits

  • Scoring

    1. Play to 21 points
    2. Whoever wins gets the point no matter who served
    3. Must win by 2
